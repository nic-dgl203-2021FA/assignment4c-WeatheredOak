BIO

In 2020 I returned to school after many years of working in various fields to build on those experiences with new skills that I can apply towards a career in design and media. This webpage is a work in progress.

I live in the Comox Valley with my wife and our cat. I enjoy music, art, history, nature, and science. I also believe a little humor goes a long way. I play guitar, percussion, and some keyboards. I do a lot of home recording, and as a hobby I create videos for my YouTube channel Weathered Oak Media.
